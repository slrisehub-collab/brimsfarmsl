" Africa does not lack resources. It lacks systems that honour those resources. Brim's Farm is that system — engineered from the ground up, built to last generations.

Brima Fayia is a visionary entrepreneur and systems thinker whose work sits at the intersection of agriculture, climate technology, and community development.
